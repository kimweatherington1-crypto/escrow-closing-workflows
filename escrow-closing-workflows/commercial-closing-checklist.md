# Commercial Real Estate Closing Checklist
**Kim Weatherington | Legal Operations Portfolio**

---

## Purpose
This checklist governs the end-to-end coordination of commercial real estate closings — from pre-closing preparation through post-closing file completion. Designed for use on commercial purchases, sales, refinances, and commercial foreclosure transactions.

---

## Phase 1 — Pre-Closing (30+ Days Out)

### Transaction Setup
- [ ] Open escrow / file — assign file number and enter all parties
- [ ] Confirm transaction type: Purchase · Sale · Refinance · Foreclosure · 1031 Exchange
- [ ] Collect and verify Purchase and Sale Agreement (PSA) — review key terms and deadlines
- [ ] Identify all parties: Buyer, Seller, Lender, Brokers, Attorneys
- [ ] Confirm entity types and obtain organizational documents (Operating Agreement, Articles, etc.)
- [ ] Confirm authorized signatories for all entities
- [ ] Order title search and review preliminary title commitment

### Title Review
- [ ] Review Schedule A — insured parties, legal description, vesting
- [ ] Review Schedule B-I — requirements to be satisfied before closing
- [ ] Review Schedule B-II — exceptions to coverage
- [ ] Flag clouds on title: liens, judgments, encumbrances, easements
- [ ] Order lien searches: UCC, tax, judgment, municipal
- [ ] Confirm HOA / CDD status and obtain estoppel if applicable
- [ ] Identify and resolve any title issues before closing date

### Due Diligence Coordination
- [ ] Track due diligence deadline from PSA
- [ ] Coordinate receipt of: survey, environmental reports, inspection reports, zoning letters
- [ ] Review and circulate to appropriate parties
- [ ] Confirm due diligence contingencies are waived or extended in writing

---

## Phase 2 — Pre-Closing (14 Days Out)

### Lender Coordination (if applicable)
- [ ] Receive and review loan commitment letter
- [ ] Confirm loan amount, rate, terms, and conditions
- [ ] Coordinate lender's closing instructions — review carefully for compliance requirements
- [ ] Order lender's title insurance policy (ALTA)
- [ ] Confirm wire instructions with lender and verify via call-back procedure
- [ ] Track all lender closing conditions and confirm satisfaction

### Document Preparation
- [ ] Prepare closing statement / settlement statement (HUD-1 or ALTA)
- [ ] Circulate draft closing statement to all parties for approval
- [ ] Prepare deed (Warranty, Special Warranty, or Quitclaim — per PSA)
- [ ] Prepare Bill of Sale if personal property is included
- [ ] Prepare Assignment of Leases and Rents (commercial)
- [ ] Prepare Seller's Affidavit, FIRPTA certification, and gap indemnity
- [ ] Prepare any required 1031 exchange documents
- [ ] Prepare closing instruction letters to all parties

### Prorations & Calculations
- [ ] Calculate real estate tax prorations (current year and any arrears)
- [ ] Calculate rent prorations (if income-producing property)
- [ ] Calculate utility and HOA prorations
- [ ] Confirm security deposit transfers
- [ ] Confirm broker commission amounts and payees
- [ ] Prepare final disbursement ledger — balance to zero

---

## Phase 3 — Closing Day

- [ ] Confirm all funds received and cleared before disbursement
- [ ] Verify wire instructions one final time before releasing any funds
- [ ] Coordinate document execution — in-person or Remote Online Notary (RON)
- [ ] Confirm all signatures, notarizations, and entity authorizations obtained
- [ ] Review executed documents for completeness before recording
- [ ] Submit deed and mortgage/lien documents for recording
- [ ] Confirm recording numbers received
- [ ] Disburse funds per approved closing statement
- [ ] Issue title insurance policies (Owner's and Lender's)

### Wire Fraud Prevention Protocol ⚠️
- [ ] NEVER send wire instructions by email without verbal confirmation
- [ ] Call back all parties on known numbers to confirm wire details
- [ ] Confirm receipt of funds before proceeding
- [ ] Document all wire verification steps in file

---

## Phase 4 — Post-Closing (Within 10 Business Days)

- [ ] Confirm all recording numbers received and entered in file
- [ ] Upload all recorded documents to file
- [ ] Prepare and distribute final closing packages to all parties
- [ ] Confirm title insurance policies issued and delivered
- [ ] Confirm all disbursements cleared
- [ ] Reconcile escrow account — confirm zero balance
- [ ] Archive file per retention schedule
- [ ] Send closing thank you / follow-up to clients and referral sources
